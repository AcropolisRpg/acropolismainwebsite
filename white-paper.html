Introducing Acropolis MMORPG

Acropolis takes its name from ancient Greek, an Acropolis was a city built in the highest part of a terrain to give military advantages, protection, scale up buildings faster, to protect and move resources between every city in the safest possible way, meaning that Acropolis MMORPG will take all those advantages and bring them to the game.

The purpose is to allow players immerse themselves in this world with the role of a Citizen of the Acropolis, trading their goods, gathering resources, fighting other players to get better resources. Then every City inside the game will have their own market based on player actions and efforts.

At Acropolis MMORPG, we believe that having unique assets, unique character and freedom, is one of the ways to make a game appealing and engaging, with the advantage of rewarding players to translate their effort to an NFT.

The intention behind Acropolis MMORPG is to build a solid decentralized economy that will be built organically

Sight and Universe of Acropolis

As of this writing, NFTs and blockchain are gaining widespread popularity across different aspects of life. and we believe this is happening for a good reason, at least for Acropolis MMORPG. NFTs are used in Acropolis MMORPG because we trust that player effort has a value that can be translated to NFTs, and of course this is something that traditional games can't bring to current market

The value lies in the effort and the ownership of these effort that every player will place in the game, allowing them to acquire in-game components. Within Acropolis MMORPG, each player will be able to build their own path, meaning they will have unique stats among every other player, and different kind of in-game components that can be traded from inside and outside of Acropolis world.

By allowing these in-game components to be moved from the inside world to the outside world players can be rewarded by the effort they invest for someone else looking to save some time, or get some unique in-game components.

Acropolis will be a Massive Multiplayer Online Role-Playing game, allowing every player to connect to each other, and bring the Acropolis to life, they will be the souls that will be moving in-game components inside the Acropolis.

Our intended Audience is every player that is looking to play, and enjoy a unique experience that will be rewarded inside and outside Acropolis MMORPG, we are looking to bring a different meaning of the current status of play to Earn allowing players to trade their goods at a free market.

Building Blocks of Acropolis

To start the game, each "future citizen" of Acropolis need to create and account and will be distributed an in-game avatar that can be upgraded to an NFT. The account will be linked to a wallet for example Metamask, where the player will store their NFTs, and ACR Tokens.

Every player will be able to create any quantity of accounts allowing only a single player by each wallet.

To purchase in-game components like, potions, armament, and any other tradable item inside the game, players will be using the official Acropolis Token (ACR).

In the initial stages of Acropolis game development, players will be able to play at their browsers, and is recommended to use a Desktop computer for the best experience. Our team will work to bring also the game for other platforms like mobile devices being the first in the list Android devices.

Biomes is one of the key pieces for Acropolis MMORPG these will be zones with different kind of resources, at the beginning Acropolis will have the next Biomes and more Biomes will be added as the game keeps growing.

  • Dessert
  • Forest
  • Frozen
  • Swamp
  • Tundra

Every Biome will have a single city, and cities will be the place for players to trade their goods meaning they will have a market place, every city will be a safe zone meaning players will not be able to attack other players inside cities.

Zones players will be able to move free around the world and every zone will have a status indicating if is a safe place or if players will be able to attack other players.

Player vs Player (PVP) every player staying in danger zone can attack other player, to steal resources, or protect their friends. if your player is killed, the 100% of the items will have a probability to be destroyed, in case they are not destroyed the enemy will be able to take those items as a reward.

Server anti-cheating system Our system is running using an Authoritative server system, meaning everything that happens inside the Acropolis is ruled by the Acropolis Servers, and every request like player movement, items transactions, will be validated at the server meaning that if the system detects some weird behavior it will flag and track those accounts, players will be able to report also unexpected behaviors and if those players are already flagged by the system they will be candidates to be exiled from the Acropolis.

In-game Economy players will be able to trade their items at every market place on every city of the Acropolis, the currency to enable trading will be the ACR Token, and they will be able to assign any price for any item inside the game that they want to publish in the market place.

Experience Pointsplayers will get Experience points for the activity they are leveling up and experience will be also tied to the Biome where they are doing their activities, for example if a Player is gathering resources at Dessert if they try to gather the same resource at other Biome they will get less of that resource if the experience was higher in the previous Biome. This rule will apply for every Resource and Item inside the game also for crafting.

Resources and Items Resources will be able to be gathered inside the world from, trees, plants, monsters and dungeons, drop will be based depending on the level of the character meaning a higher level a better drop they will get for that resource.

Game Activities

The purpose of this section is to do a quick recap of the interaction of the building blocks of Acropolis MMORPG.

Level up one of the best activities a player can do is level up their characters and specialize them for a specific activity, this will make you better at hunting, gathering or any other activity you perform. .

Gathering when leveling up you will get Resources and those resources will belong to specific biome meaning they will be harder to get in other biomes.

Trading This will be one of the essential activities of Acropolis citizens, they will be able to trade their goods in the acropolis marketplace allowing you to get other kind of useful resources.

PVP Players will be able to kill other players in dangerous zones and the same rule of experience will apply meaning they will get a better bonus if they kill or defeat other players everything inside this game is a trade, and your PVP experience will be reduced if you are killed and promoted when you kill.

Play to Earn & Free to Play

Play to earn is really new in the gaming industry and we believe that the most important and valuable thing of this model is the player itself

Model Every player and every item will be able to be crafted as NFT to be sold outside the game, and the value of every item will be based in ACR, this will apply inside the game and outside the game marketplaces.
